Display how much each of your WordPress posts are earning through GoldHat directly on the page.

== Description ==

This is the official WordPress widget for GoldHat.  By using the GoldHat widget you can display to your readers just how much GoldHat
users appreciate your great content.  The widget displays number of donations made to your post, the total amount of
donations as well as the name of the highest donor.  If your post hasn't received any donations yet, the widget provides a way for users to
quickly access the donations page for your post.

What's GoldHat all about? Check out our [Welcome Page!](http://goldhat.org/welcome "Welcome!")
== Installation ==

1. Download goldhat-widget.zip.
2. Go to your Wordpress dashboard and click "Add New" on the plugin sidebar.
3. Click upload and choose goldhat.widget.zip
4. Click "Install Now"
